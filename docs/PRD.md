# CareerPilot AI

## Problem Statement

Students preparing for placements often lack personalized guidance for:

- Resume improvement
- DSA preparation
- Interview preparation
- Progress tracking
- Study planning

Most existing platforms provide generic content instead of individualized recommendations.

---

## Proposed Solution

CareerPilot AI is a multi-agent placement preparation platform that provides:

- Resume analysis
- Personalized DSA roadmap generation
- Interview preparation plans
- Progress tracking
- Automated study scheduling
- Secure handling of personal information

---

## Target Users

- B.Tech Students
- Final Year Students
- Internship Aspirants
- Placement Preparation Candidates

---

## Core Features

### Resume Analysis

Upload resume and receive:

- Resume score
- ATS compatibility score
- Missing skills
- Improvement suggestions

### DSA Roadmap

Generate:

- Weekly plans
- Topic prioritization
- Learning milestones

### Interview Preparation

Generate:

- Technical questions
- HR questions
- Behavioral preparation

### Progress Tracking

Track:

- Completed tasks
- Readiness score
- Learning analytics

### Study Scheduling

Automatically:

- Create calendar events
- Manage study sessions

---

## Security Features

- PII masking
- Prompt injection protection

---

## Success Metrics

- Roadmap accuracy
- Resume review quality
- Interview relevance
- Security pass rate
